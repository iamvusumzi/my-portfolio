My Story

I am a self-driven computer programming enthusiast. I'm an introverted person who warms up after a short while.

My passion for tech and programming in particular came after I realised maths was my favourite subject. This was because I am an analytical person and a problem-solver. It became easier for me to develop a passion for programming since it has some mathematical thinking involved.

I find the idea of being able to learn languages that I can use to communicate with a computer so it does what I wanted it to do fascinating. As a result, I learnt my first language, Python, whilst I was doing my matric. I've since grown and still look for more opportunities to grow and upskill myself within the tech space.
